Meet The Candidate

Deep Roots. Real Results.

Sameer Tandon is a dedicated community leader, professional, and proud resident of Brampton. For over a decade, Sameer has been at the forefront of community advocacy, working tirelessly to ensure our neighborhoods are safe, inclusive, and prosperous.

As your Regional Councillor for Wards 2 & 6, Sameer brings a wealth of experience from both the private sector and grassroots organizing. He understands that Brampton's rapid growth requires smart, sustainable planning that puts residents first—not developers.

"I am running because I believe in the unmatched potential of Brampton. We need leadership that listens, acts transparently, and fights for our fair share of regional resources to improve our daily lives."

Our Platform

A Clear Vision for Wards 2 & 6

My priorities are shaped by the conversations I've had with thousands of residents at the doors. Here is what we will tackle together.

Smart Infrastructure

Tackling gridlock by expanding our road networks efficiently, fixing potholes faster, and advocating for better public transit connections across Peel Region to get you home sooner.

Community Safety

Working closely with Peel Regional Police to increase neighborhood patrols, combat auto thefts, and invest in youth programs to prevent crime before it starts.

Affordable Living

Fighting to keep property taxes low by eliminating municipal waste. Supporting responsible housing development so our children can afford to live in the city they grew up in.

Healthcare Access

Brampton's healthcare system is stretched. I will aggressively lobby the province for our fair share of hospital funding and support the expansion of local urgent care centers.

Parks & Environment

Protecting our green spaces, upgrading local parks in Wards 2 & 6 with modern amenities, and implementing sustainable waste management practices.
