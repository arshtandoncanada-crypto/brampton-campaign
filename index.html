Meet The Candidate

About Me

I believe public service is about listening, leading with integrity, and delivering real results for the people we serve.

For many years, I have built my career around helping individuals, solving complex problems, and making responsible decisions. My experience in finance, operations, and client service has taught me the importance of accountability, transparency, and putting people first.

As your Regional Councillor, my commitment is simple: to be a strong voice for our community at Peel Region and ensure every decision reflects the needs of residents, families, seniors, youth, and local businesses.

I believe leadership is not about politics — it is about service. My promise is to work hard, remain accessible, and represent every resident with honesty, respect, and dedication.

Together, we can build a stronger, safer, and more affordable community for today and for future generations.

Our Platform

My Priorities for Peel Region

Affordable Taxes

Protecting your hard-earned money.

I will champion responsible budgeting, eliminate wasteful spending, and ensure every tax dollar delivers real value for residents while keeping Regional property taxes affordable.

Affordable Housing

Helping families find a place to call home.

I will support practical solutions to increase affordable housing, reduce homelessness, and work with all levels of government to build complete, livable communities.

Safe Communities

Safety is not a luxury — it is a right.

I will support strong police services, reliable paramedic response, emergency preparedness, and community safety initiatives that help keep every neighbourhood safe.

Better Health & Seniors

Healthier communities. Better care.

I will strengthen Peel Public Health programs, improve access to mental health services, support paramedics, and ensure seniors receive the dignity, care, and respect they deserve.

Strong Local Economy

Supporting businesses. Creating opportunity.

I will advocate for reducing unnecessary red tape, supporting entrepreneurs, attracting investment, and creating an environment where businesses can grow and create good-paying jobs.

Opportunities for Youth

Investing in the next generation.

I will support youth employment, skills development, recreation, leadership opportunities, and partnerships that help young people succeed and reach their full potential.

Better Transportation

Moving people safely and efficiently.

I will advocate for better regional transit, safer roads, improved traffic flow, and transportation planning that supports families, workers, seniors, students, and local businesses.

Transparent Government

Open government. Accountable leadership.

I will promote transparent decision-making, responsible financial management, open communication, and regular public engagement so residents always know how decisions are being made.

My Commitment to You

Affordable taxes. Safe communities. Affordable housing. Better health. Strong businesses. Opportunities for youth. Better transportation. Transparent government.

Together, we will build a stronger, safer, healthier, and more affordable Peel Region.
